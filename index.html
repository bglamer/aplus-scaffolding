/ 02 — Safety & Compliance

Safety and compliance you can rely on

Safety isn’t a slogan—it’s how the job is done. We bring a consistent, documented approach to every site, so builders, site managers and homeowners have what they need without the paperwork headache.

If your builder or site supervisor needs specific documentation, just let us know when you request a quote and we’ll have it ready.
SWMS

Safe Work Method Statements

Provided as required for all relevant work, so your site documentation is covered from the start.

TBM

Toolbox meetings

On-site toolbox talks keep the team and site contacts aligned on access, hazards and the day’s plan before work begins.

CERT

Handover certificates

A formal handover certificate is provided on completion of every scaffold install—clear, documented proof it’s ready to use.
